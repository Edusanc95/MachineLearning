In Bamboo.py, it has been used the sklearn BaggingRegressor, with KNN as base estimator, and random state.

It has been used also the GradientBoostingRegressor, with the MAE criterion and the deep given by deep decision tree, 
according to each city, and a random state. 

With BaggingRegressor, it was obtained a score 25.9952 and with GradientBoostingRegressor, it was obtained 26.8582 error percentage.
